Restore normalcy in J&K, keep in mind national security: SC to Centre

The Supreme Court has asked the Centre to ensure normal life is restored in J&K and to also keep in mind national safety and security while doing so. Schools, hospitals, public transport should function properly, the SC said. The SC also asked the Union of India and J&K government to file an affidavit over petitions challenging Article 370 revocation.
